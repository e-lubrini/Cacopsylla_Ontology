By Maurice LAMBERTIE. FOREWORD

Since the work on the Hemiptera of the Gironde, by
MM. E.-R. Dubois and M. Lambertie, Catalog published in the
Proceedings of the Linnean Society of Bordeaux (volume LI, p. 151-216),
quite a large number of species have been encountered which had
escaped the research of our predecessors. Although the list of species, particularly with regard to
the Cicadines and the Psyllids, is still far from being
complete, I nevertheless believed that the time had come to
give an overview of what has been found so far, except & give
additionally the species that we will not miss
take more each year. I give today, in-a first work, the supplement to the
Catalog of Messrs. E.-R. Dubois and M. Lambertie. I particularly thank Mr. J. Pérez for putting me in
relationship with Messrs. Pandellé and Dr. Gobert, for allowing me,
