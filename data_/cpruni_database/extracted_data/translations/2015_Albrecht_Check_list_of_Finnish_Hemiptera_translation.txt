in Finland. The nomenclature follows Aukema & Rieger 1995, 1997, 1999, 2001, 2006 (Heteroptera), Séderman et al. 2009 (Fulgoromorpha et Cicadomorpha), Ossiannilsson 1992 (Psylloidea), Blackman & Eastop 2014 (Aphidoidea),
Huldén 1986 & Gertsson 1987 (Aleyrodoidea), Gertsson 2005 (Coccoidea). Synonyms used in Finland have been listed. If a species only has been met with indoors or has been accidentally, or with purpose, introduced, the name has been
marked by an asterisk following the description year. Taxonomically or otherwise unclear species have been marked
with a question-mark before the name. Muscaphis drepanosiphoides (Bérner) is listed as a good species. Species added
since the latest check-list (Albrecht & al. 2008) are denoted by an asterisk (*) before the species name. Authors! addresses: Anders Albrecht, Finnish Museum of Natural History; Veikko Rinne, Zoological Museum,
